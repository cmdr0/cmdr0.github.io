Bulletpress

  • Enter the text for your bullet, and Bulletpress takes care of thinspacing to make your bullet fit
  • Create permutations of the same bullet by creating 'chunks' on their own line
    • 'Chunks' are different ways of saying (roughly) the same thing in your bullet; e.g.: Oversaw|Led|QB'd
    • Bulletpress automatically makes every permutation from your options, measures them, thinspaces them if necessary, and returns only the bullets that land inside the last 10 pixels of the AF1206
    • Chunks should be on their own line, but should not be removed from their place in the bullet, and pieces of a chunk must be separated by a pipe
    • Example of multiple chunks, creating multiple permutations of (effectively) the same information with differing lengths:
      • Oversaw|Led|QB'd
        $22.2M|$22M
        ntwk
        update|upgrade
    • Chunks can also create an 'optional' insert by including a trailing pipe; e.g.: "optional information|"
